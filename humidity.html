Latitude vs Humidity

Conclusion:
There is no definitive relationship between the latitude
of a city and the overall humidity the city experiences on any
give day. However, there seems to be a higher concentration of
cities whose humidity is close to 100%. Further data collection
and processing is recommended for a more robust analysis.
