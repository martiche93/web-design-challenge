Latitude vs Windiness

Conclusion:
There is no definitive relationship between the latitude
of a city and the overall wind speed the city may experience on
any given day. The data collected from cities without high wind speed
does seem to be more densely concentrated. However, this may have
been caused by a lack of data in high wind cities. Further data
colelction and processing is recomended for a more robust analysis.
