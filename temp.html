Latitude vs Maximum Temperature

Conclusion:
As the latitude becomes higher, the maximum temperature of the
city decreases. The closer to zero latitude, the warmer the
temperature. This graph shows that the Southern Hemisphere
latitudes experience warmer temperatures on average compared to
the Northern Hemisphere. Further data collection and processing
is recommended for a more robust analysis.
