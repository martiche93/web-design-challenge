Latitude vs Windiness

There seems to be no definitive relationship between a cities latitude and the overall speed of wind the city may experience on any given day. The data pulled from cities without very high wind speed does seem to be more densely concentrated, however, but this may have to do with not enough data in high wind cities pulled for analysis. Further data collection and processing using different variables is recommended for a more robust analysis.
