Latitude vs Cloudiness

There seems to be no definitive relationship between a cities latitude and the overall cloudiness the city may experience on any given day. Cloudiness must be more related to the specific climate that each city has, rather than the set latitude. Further data collection and processing using different variables is recommended for a more robust analysis.
