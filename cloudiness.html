Latitude vs Cloudiness

Conclusion:
There is no definitive relationship between the latitude of a city
and the overall cloudiness the city may experience on any given day.
Cloudiness must be more related to the specific climate that each city has,
rather than the actual latitude. Further data collection and processing
is recommended for a more robust analysis.
